How AdPilot works

Step 1 — Quick Q&A

Answer five simple questions: business type, goal, location, target customers and budget. We translate these into campaign inputs.

Step 2 — AI Strategy

AdPilot recommends the best platforms and budget split, explained in plain language so you can decide confidently.

Step 3 — 1-Click Designer

Auto-generate creatives for Reels, Stories, Posts and Search ads. Edit text or images quickly and preview.

Step 4 — Launch & Optimize

Simulate launch or export campaign kits. Monitor performance in the intuitive dashboard and apply AI suggestions to improve results.
